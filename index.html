StackAdapt is one of the fastest-growing tech startups in Toronto, with an innovative native advertising platform that enables brands to accelerate customer engagement and acquisition.

As the company grew in size and reach, their ambitions for a world-class online presence grew as well. They hired me as a Front End Engineer to give much-needed focus to their homepage and blog.

I worked closely with the Design and Marketing teams to revamp their homepage, introducing new product pages, industry pages tailored to specific verticals, an Ad Format Showcase, and a lot more.

One of my first initiatives was a migration of their old blog to WordPress, including the development of a custom WordPress theme.

I also built responsive email templates, landing pages and other tools for their marketing automation platform, which helped to elevate their communication and customer experience to new heights.
